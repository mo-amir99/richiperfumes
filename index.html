About Our Products

It is a collection of high quality and concentrated perfumes, As each of our products is the result of the efforts of French perfumers. They are EDP products "Eau Du Parfum", In other words the concentration of perfume oil in the product is not less than 25%, and this leads to the stability of the perfume on the clothes and the skin throughout the day, with the gradation of the perfume trend during the day according to the aromatic pyramid for each type of oil. The types of essential oils used in RICHI Perfumes are absolutely the best in terms of concentration, the quality of the natural oils used and the type of solvent used in the DPG oil "Dipropylene Glycol". It is the only solvent approved globally by WHO and IFRA "International Fragrances Association"
